About Ritaj

Welcome to Ritaj Restaurant, where tradition meets excellence. Located in the heart of Abu Dhabi, we bring you the finest Indian cuisine crafted with passion and authenticity.

Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes to create dishes that transport you to the rich culinary heritage of India. Every meal is a celebration of flavor, culture, and hospitality.

Whether you're joining us for a family gathering, business lunch, or romantic dinner, Ritaj offers an unforgettable dining experience in an elegant atmosphere.
